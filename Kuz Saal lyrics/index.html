About the Song

Kuz Saal is the newest Punjabi song from the album “Chobar.” It is sung by Arjan Dhillon and features Charnpreet Dhillon. The music is created by Mxrci, while Arjan Dhillon wrote the lyrics. The music video, directed by Ravan Khosa, has been released by Brown Studios.

About the Artist

Arjan Dhillon is a talented Punjabi singer, songwriter, and lyricist known for his ability to bring raw emotions and deep storytelling into his music. With songs like "Tape," "Jalwa," and "Gutt," he has established himself as one of the most influential artists in modern Punjabi music. His music beautifully balances tradition and modernity, gaining him a loyal fanbase worldwide.

Released in 2023. Produced by Mxrci.
